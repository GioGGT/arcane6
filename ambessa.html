Ambessa

Noxus and Piltover

   Ambessa, a cunning and influential character in Arcane, is a high-ranking member of Piltover's ruling council and the mother of Mel Medarda. Known for her political acumen and ability to manipulate the power dynamics of the city, Ambessa plays a crucial role in shaping Piltover's future. Her calculated decisions and ambitious nature make her a formidable force in the city's politics, and she is unafraid to use her wealth and influence to secure her family's position. As a mother, Ambessa’s relationship with Mel is complex, shaped by her expectations and the pressure she places on her daughter to uphold the family’s legacy.
